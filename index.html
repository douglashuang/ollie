Ollie 🐾

Everything you need to take great care of him

🚨 Top 5 Rules — Read First

These are non-negotiable

  1. If Ollie is not being actively watched → put him in the crate
  2. 🦮 Always leash Ollie indoors
  3. ❌ Do NOT let him jump on or off furniture or high surfaces
  4. 🍽 Do NOT feed more than 8 spoons total per day
  5. 🐕 No marking and non-potty/poo related smelling

📅 Daily Schedule

  • 7–10 AM Wake up — whenever you wake up
  • 8:00 AM Breakfast
  • After breakfast Morning potty — typically on the balcony; poo is optional
  • 1–2 PM Afternoon potty (optional, if you're home)
  • 5:00 PM Evening potty — only if there was no afternoon potty
  • 6:00 PM Dinner
  • After dinner After-dinner potty
  • 9:00 PM Last potty of the night
  • ~11:00 PM Bedtime — brush if you remember

🍽 Feeding

⚠️ Max 8 tablespoons total per day

This includes meals and rewards. Do not exceed this regardless of begging.

Meals

  • Morning & evening: 2 tablespoons of kibble each
  • Evening meal only: also include a Nuvet tablet
  • Remaining kibble throughout the day is for rewards only

Mealtime Routine

  1. Prepare food — Ollie must sit to the side while you prepare it
  2. Place bowl down and say "ok" to release him to eat
  3. ❌ Do NOT reward bad behavior with food

💧 Water

  • Ollie typically wants to drink water around 11:00 AM — if nobody is home then, try to get him to drink before crating, or give him an ice cube
  • In the evening he'll drink freely, especially after a collagen bone

🚶 Walk Routine

Before You Go

Use the harness, not the collar, for walks.

When NOT to walk

  • ❌ Raining
  • ❌ Too hot outside

Walk Rules

  • ❌ No marking or excessive sniffing — sniffing is only allowed on grass for potty and poo
  • ❌ Do not let him greet every dog
  • Command him to go potty immediately at the start of the walk
  • Walks are normally 15 minutes — on longer walks, he may go again at the end
  • If he's very excited or happy, he may poo twice or more — bring extra bags

If Ollie Stops Walking

  • ❌ Do NOT pick him up
  • Say "come on" or "let's go"
  • ✓ Reward with 1 kibble when he continues

🧼 After the Walk

Ask for "paw" to wipe his feet. He may be hyper — to calm him down:

  1. Say "place" → send him to his spot
  2. Say "down" → lie down
  3. ✓ Reward with 1 kibble

🌿 Potty

Off-Walk Potty

Any time Ollie needs to go outside of a scheduled walk, take him to an outdoor setting — e.g. a balcony. Do not let him go indoors.

Commands

  • Say "potty" → he should pee
  • Say "poo" → he should poop
After command: stand still in one spot, wait ~1 minute. If nothing happens → continue walking.
🌿 Ollie only goes on grass patches or pee pads — guide him to one before giving the command.

⚠️ Potty Accidents

Watch for these triggers — act before an accident happens:

  • Too hyper / overstimulated
  • Drank a lot of water recently
  • Just came out of a long crate session
  • Sitting by / pawing at the balcony door
If he does have an accident: immediately take him to the potty area and say "go potty" — don't wait, don't scold, just redirect.

✅ Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do's

  • Speak in a calm tone around him
  • Reward good behavior occasionally
  • Ignore him when whining and pawing from the crate — if his needs have been met

❌ Don'ts

  • Aggressive play will wind him up and make him hyper — keep play calm
  • Anything within Ollie's reach he will try to chew or play with — keep bags, controllers, socks, and similar items out of range

🏠 House Rules

  • Always keep the leash on indoors
  • ❌ No jumping on or off furniture
  • Not supervised → crate
  • You're busy → crate
  • Only reward good behavior

📦 Crate Rules

  1. Say "crate"
  2. Reward with 1 kibble once he's inside
Use the crate any time nobody is watching him or you're occupied with something else.

🧠 Command Cheat Sheet

Tap any command to see what it means.

sit Sit

Sit calmly before any action (meals, doorways, treats).

down Down

Lie down in a calm position.

wait Wait

Pause and hold position until released with "ok".

stay Stay

Hold his current position until you return or release him.

come Come / Come Back

Return to you immediately.

let's go Let's Go

Resume walking. Use when he stops on a walk.

place Place

Go to his designated spot (bed/mat). Follow with "down" to settle.

crate Crate

Go into his crate. Reward with 1 kibble once inside.

paw Paw

Lift a paw. Used after walks to wipe his feet.

go potty Go Potty / Go Poo

Eliminate on command. Stand still and wait ~1 min after saying it.

off Off / No Jump

Get off a surface or stop jumping on people or furniture.

no No

General correction — stop what you're doing.

no more No More

This activity or treats are finished. Used to clearly end something.

no bite No Bite

Stop mouthing or nipping.

good dog Good Dog / Bad Dog

Behavior markers. "Good dog" reinforces correct behavior; "bad dog" marks an unwanted action.

rollover Rollover

Fun trick — roll over onto his back.

take it Take It

Ollie will take a treat gently from your hand.

trade Trade

Ollie will give up something he's holding (e.g. a bone) in exchange for a treat.
